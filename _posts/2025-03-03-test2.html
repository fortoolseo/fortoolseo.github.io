You need links from sites that Google already trusts.</p></li><li><p data-path-to-node="13,3,0"><b>No Context:</b> True, valuable links are surrounded by relevant, informative content (contextual links). Automated generators often place links in static lists or profile pages with no context, providing no topical relevance.</p></li></ol><p data-path-to-node="14"><b>The take-away:</b> Never use a backlink generator to <i>automatically</i> build links.
