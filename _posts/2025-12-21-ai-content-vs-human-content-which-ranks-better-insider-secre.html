AI Content vs Human Content: Which Ranks Better?: Insider Secrets Revealed

December 21, 2025 SEO 5 min read

Introduction

When it comes to creating content for your website or social media channels, you're likely torn between writing it yourself or hiring someone to do it for you. And if you're not, well, you're about to be. AI content has been all the rage lately, with many claiming it's the future of content creation. But the question remains: does AI content rank better than human content? In this post, we'll dive into the world of AI content and human content to find out which one comes out on top.

But before we get started, let me ask you this: do you really want to rely on a machine to create content for your brand? I mean, think about it. A machine can generate thousands of words in a matter of seconds, but can it truly capture the essence of your brand's voice and personality? The answer might surprise you.

Why it Matters

So, why does it even matter whether your content is created by a human or a machine? The reality is, your content is the first impression people get of your brand. If it's subpar, they'll likely click away and forget about you. But if it's engaging, informative, and resonates with your audience, they'll be hooked. And that's where AI content falls short.

Here's the thing: AI content is great for quickly generating large amounts of content, but it's not great at creating high-quality, engaging content that resonates with your audience. And that's because AI content lacks the nuance and emotional intelligence that a human can bring to the table.

But don't just take my word for it. A study by WordStream found that 70% of marketers believe that AI-generated content is less effective than human-generated content. And that's not all - they also found that 63% of marketers believe that AI-generated content is less trustworthy than human-generated content.

How to Do it

So, how can you create high-quality, engaging content that resonates with your audience? Here are a few tips to get you started:

Write from the Heart

When writing for your brand, it's essential to write from the heart. This means being authentic, vulnerable, and true to your brand's voice and personality. AI content can't do this, no matter how hard it tries.

Here are some questions to ask yourself when writing from the heart:

  • What are my brand's values and mission?
  • What sets my brand apart from the competition?
  • What do I want my audience to feel when they read my content?

Use Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand and connect with your audience on an emotional level. And let me tell you, AI content can't do this. AI content is all about data and analytics, but human content is about connecting with people on a deeper level.

Here are some ways to use emotional intelligence in your content:

  • Use storytelling techniques to connect with your audience.
  • Use emotional language to convey your message.
  • Use imagery and visuals to evoke emotions.

Make it Personal

Finally, make your content personal. This means sharing your own experiences, insights, and expertise. AI content can't do this, no matter how hard it tries.

Here are some ways to make your content personal:

  • Share your own stories and anecdotes.
  • Share your own expertise and insights.
  • Use humor and personality in your writing.

Key Takeaways

So, what can we take away from this? Here are a few key takeaways:

1. AI content is great for quickly generating large amounts of content, but it's not great at creating high-quality, engaging content that resonates with your audience.

2. Human content is more effective at creating high-quality, engaging content that resonates with your audience.

3. To create high-quality, engaging content, you need to write from the heart, use emotional intelligence, and make it personal.

Conclusion

So, there you have it. AI content vs human content: which one ranks better? The answer is clear: human content. But it's not just about creating content that ranks better - it's about creating content that resonates with your audience and builds a loyal following. And that's something that AI content can never do.

So, the next time you're considering creating content for your brand, remember: it's not just about the words on the page - it's about the person behind them. And that person is you.
