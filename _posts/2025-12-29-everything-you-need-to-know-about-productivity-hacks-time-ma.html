How to Do it

Time Management: The Basics

Time management is the foundation of productivity. When you can manage your time effectively, you can get more done in less time and reduce stress. Here are a few basic principles to get you started:

  • Use a planner or calendar to schedule your day
  • Set clear goals and priorities
  • Break down large tasks into smaller, manageable chunks
  • Use time-blocking to schedule focused work time

Here's a great resource to get you started: Calendly is a tool that helps you schedule meetings and appointments without back-and-forth email conversations.

Notion Templates: Streamlining Your Workflow

Notion is a powerful tool for organizing your thoughts, tasks, and projects. With a Notion template, you can create a customized system that works for you. Here are a few tips for getting started:

  • Start with a blank slate and build your template from scratch
  • Use templates to create reusable pages and databases
  • Customize your template to fit your unique needs and workflow

Here's a great resource to check out: Notion's Template Gallery is a treasure trove of pre-built templates to get you started.

So what are the key takeaways from our productivity hacks discussion? Here are a few key points to remember:
