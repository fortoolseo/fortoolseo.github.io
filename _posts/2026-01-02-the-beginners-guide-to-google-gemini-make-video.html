How to Do it

Step 1: Set Up Your Google Gemini Account

Before you can start creating videos with Google Gemini, you'll need to set up your account. Head over to the Google Gemini website and sign in with your Google account. Click on the "Get started" button, and follow the prompts to create a new project.

Make sure to choose the "Video" option, as this is where you'll be creating your video.

Step 2: Input Your Ideas

Once your project is set up, it's time to input your ideas. Google Gemini uses a conversational interface, so simply type away what you want to say, and it'll take care of the rest.
