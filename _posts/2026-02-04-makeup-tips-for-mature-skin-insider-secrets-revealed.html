How to Do it

Primer: The Secret to a Smooth Canvas

When it comes to mature skin, primer is your BFF. It helps to create a smooth, even surface for your makeup to adhere to, which is especially important if you're dealing with fine lines and wrinkles. Look for a primer that contains hyaluronic acid or silicones, as these will help to lock in moisture and create a barrier against dryness.

Here are some key things to look for in a primer:

  • Silicone-based primers are great for smoothing out fine lines and wrinkles
  • Hyaluronic acid-based primers are perfect for locking in moisture and reducing dryness
  • Primers with SPF can also help to protect your skin from the sun

Foundation: Finding the Right Shade

Finding the right foundation can be tricky, but it's especially important when it comes to mature skin. You want to look for a shade that matches your skin tone, but also takes into account any areas of discoloration or unevenness. If you're struggling to find a shade that matches, consider getting a color match at a makeup counter or using a foundation color corrector to help even out your skin tone.

Here are some key things to look for in a foundation:

  • Look for a foundation that contains antioxidants and SPF to help protect your skin from the sun
  • Choose a foundation with a matte finish to help reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles
  • Consider a foundation with a built-in primer to help create a smooth, even surface

Highlight and Contour: Adding Dimension

Highlight and contour are two of the most powerful tools in your makeup bag when it comes to adding dimension to mature skin. By applying highlight to the high points of your face (such as your cheekbones and nose), and contour to the low points (such as your hollows and temples), you can create a more youthful, radiant appearance.

Here are some key things to keep in mind when it comes to highlight and contour:

  • Use a light hand when applying highlight, as it's easy to overdo it
  • Choose a contour shade that's one to two shades deeper than your foundation
  • Blend, blend, blend! Make sure to blend your highlight and contour well to avoid any harsh lines

Key Takeaways

So, what are the key takeaways from today's post? Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Highlight and contour can add dimension to your face and make you look more youthful
